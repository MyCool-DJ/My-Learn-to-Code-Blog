WORDPRESS

A CSM May 19th, 2021

A CMS industry standard in building Websites!

WordPress is a free and open-source content management system written in PHP and paired with a MySQL (I am learning SQL on my MIMO app) or MariaDB database. Features include a plugin architecture (Like DIVI a drop and drag website builder I used for a startup during my degree) and a template system (just past in your content and photos/videos etc), referred to within WordPress as Themes. I have made my website for my Assignment 1 in this CMS and I will contiune to learn how to do this VIA YouTube videos and following the WordPress Groups like this one on Facebook.
