LinkedIn

There are badges you can earn if you do the LinkedIN course, April 27, 2021

Life-long learning! It is now impossible to study something and stay employed!

Many companies, especially startups (new companies), have stopped hiring staff; instead, you are hired as contractors and classed as self-employed. No longer do most companies train people; instead, you are employed on an as and when needed basis, and then it's up to you between gigs (contracts) to keep upskilling to stay employable. LinkedIn badges seem like they will become an industry standard for staying upskilled. However, it doesn't work for me, and I prefer my gamified learning apps like Mimo. I can upload my certificates (these learning to code apps have their certificates) instead of LinkedIn's credentials tab. Recruiters do look for you this way and will use AI to scan to see if you have the credentials needed for a job. Applying for jobs is becoming a thing of the past. This is the gig economy and is the new normal in the 4th industrial revolution. You have to be a lifelong learner.
